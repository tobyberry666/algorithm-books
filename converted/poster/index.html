In ACW1, I chose to become a Computer Science teacher because I am passionate

about technology and problem-solving. I also enjoy helping others learn and believe

that education can have a positive impact on people's lives. For me, this career

combines my interest in computer science with my desire to inspire future learners.

ACW2 strengthened my understanding of this career choice. One of the most valuable

lessons was learning about effective teaching strategies. I realized that good teaching is

not only about sharing knowledge but also about engaging students and supporting

different learning needs. This helped me appreciate the skills required to become an

effective educator.

Another important insight was the connection between computer science and the real

world. Technology influences many aspects of society, and computer science education

plays an important role in preparing students for future challenges and opportunities.

This reinforced my belief that teaching computer science is both meaningful and

valuable.

After reflecting on what I learned in ACW2, my career choice has been reinforced. The

module did not change my direction; instead, it strengthened my confidence in

becoming a Computer Science teacher. I now have a clearer understanding of the

responsibilities and challenges of the profession, and I feel more motivated to pursue

this path.

Looking ahead, I plan to continue developing my technical knowledge, gain practical

experience, and improve my teaching skills. My goal is to create an engaging learning

environment where students can develop confidence, creativity, and problem-solving

abilities.

In conclusion, ACW2 has strengthened my commitment to becoming a Computer

Science teacher. The knowledge and experiences gained from the module have

confirmed that this career aligns with my interests, values, and future goals.
